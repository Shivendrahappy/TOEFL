The paragraph says:

scientists identified the impact from a worldwide sediment layer
that layer had iridium
iridium is rare on Earth but common in meteorites

So the evidence was:

the unusual sediment layer

That directly matches:

✅ B) They found a unique layer of sediment worldwide.

Why A is wrong:

The crater was found later.

The passage says:

first they identified impact from sediment
later geologists located crater in Mexico

The paragraph gives a timeline:

Scientists first identified impact from sediment layer
Later they found crater in Yucatán

So:

❌ A) “They discovered a large crater…”

Wrong because:

crater was found later
question asks HOW they determined the impact initially

✅ B) “They found a unique layer of sediment worldwide.”

Correct because:

paragraph explicitly says:

“Scientists first identified this impact in 1980 from the worldwide layer of sediment…”

This directly answers the question.

❌ C) “They were alerted by archaeologists…”

Wrong because:

Archaeologists are NEVER mentioned
TOEFL often inserts believable but nonexistent details

❌ D) “They located a meteorite…”

Wrong because:

they never found meteorite itself
they found evidence of impact
